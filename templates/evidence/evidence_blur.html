How it works
  1. Faces are highlighted with red boxes (will be blurred)
  2. Tap a box to mark it as the student — it turns green (kept clear)
  3. Drag a box to reposition it
  4. Drag the corners to resize
  5. Press the × button to delete a box
  6. Click Add Box then draw on the image to add a new blur area
  7. Click Apply Blur & Save when ready

For freehand areas, use the Manual Blur Brush after saving.
